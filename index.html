Mnemonic Metrics

Energy (E)
ℹ️
-
Attention & Presence
How It's Calculated:

Energy combines engagement volume (how much content) and participation equity (how balanced voices are). We measure speaker/section balance using entropy — perfectly balanced participation scores high, dominated conversations score low.

Why It Matters:

High energy means people are invested and present. Low energy signals disengagement, performative participation, or unbalanced power. In meetings, it shows whose voices dominate. In texts, it reveals conceptual density.

Symbolic Coherence (s)
ℹ️
-
Meaning Density
How It's Calculated:

Symbolic coherence measures lexical diversity (vocabulary richness), conceptual depth (meaningful vs superficial language), and applies a jargon penalty for buzzwords that obscure meaning.

Why It Matters:

High coherence means the text carries clear, dense meaning. Low coherence suggests fragmentation, unclear purpose, or reliance on empty jargon. This reveals whether ideas hold together or drift apart.

Connection² (c²)
ℹ️
-
Speed × Trust
How It's Calculated:

Connection measures relational clarity (absence of misreads, clarifications, disagreements) and conversational velocity (how fast ideas flow without getting stuck in questions). High questions = slower connection.

Why It Matters:

High connection means trust, clear communication, and appropriate pace. Low connection signals either speed too high OR trust too low. This is squared in the equation because velocity effects are non-linear — small speed changes create big impacts.

Impact (I)
ℹ️
-
Mnemonic Residue
How It's Calculated:

I = (E · s) / c²
Impact combines all three dimensions. High energy and coherence can only create lasting impact if connection is strong. Low connection (in denominator) means even good content creates weak mnemonic residue.

Why It Matters:

This measures what endures in memory. High impact means this conversation/text will be remembered and will shape future thinking. Low impact means it fades or leaves negative traces. This is what the system leaves behind.
